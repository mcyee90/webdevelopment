Latitude vs. Windspeed

Windspeed seens to be closer to bi-modal than many of the other datasets, with higher windspeeds nearer to the poles and lower windspeeds nearer to the equator. This makes sense given the warmer climates nearer to the equator and more mild weather conditions, although this trend is not as pronounced as the temperature trend. Overall, it appears that windspeeds for most cities are around 0-10 mph.
