Latitude vs. Humidity

Humidity also seems to vary with respect to the location on the map. There is a large cluster of data near the equator with 100% humidity. This is understandable given that many warm, tropical climates and cities reside near the equator. What's odd is that there seems to be another large cluster of cities north of the equator in the 45-70 degrees latitude range that has 100% humidity. The same range south of the equator does not mirror this trend. This may indicate that this trend is seasonal and we may expect a reversal in the trend when the southern hemisphere is in its warmer seasons.
